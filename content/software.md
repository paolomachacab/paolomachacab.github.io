## Projects
- **[Atlas Municipal ODS Bolivia 2026](https://paolomachacab.github.io/atlas_sdsn_paolo/)** -- *In collaboration with [SDSN Bolivia](https://sdsnbolivia.org/)* <details> <summary>[Description]</summary> Interactive web application for the territorial visualization of Sustainable Development Goal (SDG) indicators across the 343 municipalities of Bolivia, comparing the evolution of development outcomes between two time periods (2012–2024). The Atlas includes municipal-level maps, a population-weighted cartogram, a Sustainable Development Index (IDS) ranking, and β-convergence analysis across all 17 SDGs. This visualization tool was used during the external validation workshop of the Atlas.

  <small>*All information, data, and visualizations contained in this Atlas are the exclusive property of [SDSN Bolivia](https://sdsnbolivia.org/), produced in collaboration with academic and governmental institutions. © SDSN Bolivia 2026.*</small> </details>
