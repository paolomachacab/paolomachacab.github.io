## Projects

- **[Atlas Municipal ODS Bolivia 2026](https://paolomachacab.github.io/atlas_sdsn_paolo/)** -- *In collaboration with [SDSN Bolivia](https://sdsnbolivia.org/)* <details> <summary>[Description]</summary> Interactive web application for the territorial visualization of Sustainable Development Goal (SDG) indicators across the 343 municipalities of Bolivia, comparing the evolution of development outcomes between two time periods (2012–2024). The Atlas includes municipal-level maps, a population-weighted cartogram, a Sustainable Development Index (IDS) ranking, and β-convergence analysis across all 17 SDGs. </details>
